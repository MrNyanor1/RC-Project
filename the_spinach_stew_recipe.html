The Recipe: Spinach Stew

Hello, my name is Tom, and I'm a professional Chef of the restaurant. Today we're going to make spinach stew. Here are the ingredients below.

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Roma Tomatoes
  • At least two Pink Salmons
  • Remie Chicken Flavor
  • Chopped Spinach
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomato Paste

Instructions

  1. Get a wooden cutting board and get tomatoes and onions and put them on it.
    2.  
  2. Cut the onions on the wooden cutting board (PLEASE NOTE: When the onions get to your eye and you're not comfortable, you stop and take 2-minute break).
    3.  
  3. Do the same with tomatoes.
    4.  
  4. Put them on their plates.
    5.  
  5. Get a big pan, and make sure it's properly clean before and after use.
    6.  
  6. Turn on the stove, and after that, put the olive oil in the pan.
    7.  
  7. When you see the tiny bubbles, put the onions first in the pan.
    8.  
  8. Stir the onions every 5 minutes till the onions turn yellow
    9.  
  9. Put the tomatoes in and do the same as the previous step.
    10.  
  10. While doing the previous step (step 9), cut the chopped spinach open and put it on the plate and then put it in the microwave for around at least 3 minutes.
    11.  
  11. While you wait for the spinach to get enough heat, get the tomato paste, open it, and put it in the pan and stir or mix it.
    12.  
  12. The Remie Chicken Flavor is optional, but it tastes better when you add it to the pan and stir as well.
    13.  
  13. After the microwave is done, you put the spinach in the pan and mix it.
    14.  
  14. Add a little amount of salt in the pan and mix it (PLEASE NOTE: Make sure you lower the stove).
    15.  
  15. Add the Pink Salmons in the pan and cut them into pieces
    16.  
  16. Wait for about 15-20 minutes for the spinach stew to be ready.
